ABOUT US

You Obviously Need This is a place to find tchotchkies for friends, family, and anyone in your life who has everything. With a very unique and highly curated bunch of finds, there is always something new to inspire.

Our little online shop was born out of a love for vintage and the unseen treasures that come home with us from our world travels. We pick everything in the shop by hand, with the greatest attention to detail, from what it's made of and where it's made, to who made it and how it will help them in their little corner of the world.

We hope you enjoy our unique products, brought to you from us!
